Define Product

What is Disocvery for Supplier and
why does an analytical page matter to them?

Discovery helps suppliers increase visibility and connect with global buyers by showcasing verified profiles, certifications, and product offerings. Through smart matching and search filters, suppliers are invited to relevant sourcing events, while insights help them refine their strategy and win more business.

An analytical page matters to suppliers because it provides clear visibility into how they’re performing on the platform. With insights into conversion rates, engagement levels, regional interest, product categories, and keyword trends, suppliers can understand buyer behavior and optimize their profiles accordingly. The recommendations feature offers actionable tips to improve discoverability and competitiveness—making it a strategic tool for attracting the right buyers and increasing business opportunities.

Summary

As a senior UX designer, this project taught me how to turn complex data into clear, actionable insights that support supplier goals. I learned to design for data literacy, embed meaningful recommendations, and create scalable layouts that adapt to different user needs. It also deepened my skills in cross-functional collaboration and reinforced the importance of aligning UX with measurable business outcomes.

Key Learnings and Future Improvements

  • Simplified complex data into clear, actionable insights tailored to supplier needs.

  • Strengthened cross-functional collaboration to align design with data and business goals.

  • Built scalable, user-friendly layouts with room for interactivity and deeper exploration.

  • Identified future enhancements, including benchmarking, alerts, and accessibility improvements.
