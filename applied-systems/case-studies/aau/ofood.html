OFood



What

Watch the relationship between food and environment. According to the project, it helps people to understand how a person can do to protect the environment from the eating habit.

How

The app analyzes how the environment affects health, and how people can change food shopping habits to change the environment. Protecting the environment starts with having organic food.

Why

OFood is for people want to know more about organic food and want to have a healthy life both outside and inside. Outside, it is about the environment.

How is this unique?



It educates people about organic food
It offers organic food recipe
It shows the farmers market schedule every week
It has nutritionist tips for healthy.

How does it help?



It helps people to understand the relationship between food and environment
It offers the users the location of the organic food market nearby
It teaches the users How to protect environment indirectly
It makes the users know their body and physical condition

Research



Why people choose organic food?

55% healthy eating

35% taste of organic food

53% avoiding chemical residues

31% animal welfare

44% care for the environment and nature
