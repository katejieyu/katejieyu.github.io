USPACE



What

Uspace is a mobile app to help users pick matching furniture within their budget by virtually furnishing their rooms.

How

A virtual room is created by taking pictures of the room to be furnished. The app has a library of furniture from different companies. The furniture can be dragged into the virtual room to be previewed. Users are able to order the furniture in the once satisfied with the design.

Why

This App allows users to compare different styles before placing the order, saving time and money by eliminating wrong purchases. The App also suggests different styles to give ideas to the user.

How is this unique?



This app helps people to buy furniture within their budget. There are some apps and websites that allow users to see different furniture and check their prices but no solution exists to see the furniture in their own rooms. The users can use Uspace to see the finished look and compare different styles immediately, saving time and money.

How does it help?



Save money by shopping online
Reduce waste of money on the wrong furniture
Save trips to the local store and pick up the furniture
Gives users a preview of the room with their picked furniture
Design style by users
