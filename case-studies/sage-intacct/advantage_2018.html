Sage Intacct
Advantage 2018



What

This is an app for Sage Intacct Anual traditional event called advantage 2018. It hosts at the Gaylord Opryland Resort and Convention Center. Over 3000 people join the event including customers, partners, speakers, and employees.

How

The app helps over 3000 users to know their schedule in 5 days event. At the same time, they have their own unique username and password to take a look every sessions, speakers, sponsors, Maps. and every infomation.

Why

With the app, people joining the advantage event don't need any booklets or guidelines to follow up this event. The app reminds every registed events on time. It can be called advantage event walking dictionary.

How is this unique?



It is built for just Sage Intacct advantage 2018 event
It contains every informstion about the event
It supoorts eat, live, and classes sessions
It has a data collection suport for registration.

How does it help?



It helps people to follow up the schedule of the event
It offers the users the location of session rooms, hotel rooms, and anywhere you can go in the hotel
It supports the users to send and recieve message in this app
It reminds the users their registed sessions when the sessions are coming
