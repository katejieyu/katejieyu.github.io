EverDream



What

Busy people need to take a break. Everdream lets people know different cultures, and essentially gives them a free trip. Allows people a peaceful space to calm down.

How

To bring people what they want in life by using a 360 degree projector and connecting with a smart phone to play different scenarios on the wall with their chosen music and sounds.

Why

People are stressed from work and don’t have time to explore the world and different cultures. They don’t have enough money to go far for trips, and need peaceful environment to charge their minds.

How is this unique?



This is a product with a 360 degree projector and a connect app. The 360 degree projector needs to be installed on top of the roof and projector is projected on the wall. The app connects with the projector and picks the enviroment that the user wants on the wall. The user may just be at home, but they can be transported anywhere they’d like to be. They can pick the right senario, music and sound for their mood.

How does it help?



Reduce stress from daily life
Save the money from the trip
Create a ideal environment
Be relax anytime
Be flexible to control the senario

HOW IT WORKS



Pair the projector to the mobile EverDream app

Set up the projects in a personal smart phone

Buy and Download the resouces of senarios, musics, and sounds in EverDream store online

Pick a time, senario, music, and sound to play in the play list

Play a 3D senario with music, sounds in the room and enjoy the time

MAIN IDEA

The app works with EverDream 3D projector to create a ideal room to have fun or be relax as the users' setting.
